Installing the program

To install sBotics you'll have to download our launcher in our official website.

Windows

Skip to Configuring launcher.

MacOS

We are currently experimenting with full MacOS launcher support, and thus sadly the simulator has to be redownloaded manually each and every update. First download the application from our website:

Linux

On linux open the terminal after downloading sBotics, cd to the download folder and execute the following commands: chmod +x sBotics.AppImage Then, exceute the AppImage: sBotics.AppImage

Configuring launcher

After opening the launcher select your language on the settings menu.

Click update to download the simulator's files.

Click play to open the simulator.

Authentication

Authenticate using the Person icon: .

Now you can program and use most of the simulator's features.

Generating new arena

Open the simulation settings on the sidebar: .
Now open the arena settings as shown below in the simulation settings' sidebar:

Now click on "generate new arena" in one of the desired arena formats to create a new arena. You can also change the difficulty and a few small tweaks from this screen.

Choosing robot

Open the simulation settings on the sidebar: .
Now use the arrow buttons to navigate through the robots and its actuators:

Choosing a programming language

To choose a programing language open the programming panel present in the sidebar: .
Now wait for your programs to load and click the "rE", "C#" or Blockly symbol on the right sidebar, seen below as "C#".
